I am **Haoqi He (赫浩锜)**, a researcher at **Sangfor Innovation Research Institute**, working on **LLM post-training**, **agent safety**, and **trustworthy AI** — building systems that remain useful when tools, context, and evaluators are imperfect.

Previously, I worked on **AI safety at ByteDance** (Sep 2025 – Jun 2026), with an emphasis on agents, evaluation, and adversarial failure modes. I completed my **B.Eng. in Cybersecurity** at **Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU)** in June 2026.

---

## Research Interests

I study how language-model agents can learn and act under operational constraints: partial observability, untrusted context, fallible graders, tool failures, and limited verification budgets. My current emphasis connects post-training with the harness around the model — routers, judges, evidence guards, fallback execution, and evaluation.

- **Agentic post-training** — Agentic RL, verifier-aware learning, credit assignment
- **Harness & evaluation** — Judges & graders, selective execution, evidence provenance
- **Agent security** — Prompt injection, tool-use safety, agent for security
