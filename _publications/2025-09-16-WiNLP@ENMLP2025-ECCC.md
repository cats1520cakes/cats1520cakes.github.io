Background : Cloud-based code agents risk leaking source code; pure on-device LLMs lag in capability.
• Innovation: local agent handles anonymisation and unit testing, while a cloud LLM drafts code.
• Results: Pass@1 90.0 % on HumanEval with 10 / 10 privacy score and lower latency on a RTX 3090.
