ECCC is an edge-cloud hybrid framework that uses a lightweight local model, AST-based anonymization, and edge-side privacy validation before delegating abstracted tasks to a cloud model. The peer-reviewed paper reports correctness within 4–5 percentage points of the evaluated GPT-4-based frameworks and a 10/10 privacy score under its stated evaluation.
